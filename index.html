Automatic Pet Feeder

Automatic Pet Feeder. IoT device that can Automatically feed your pet.

Automatic Pet Feeder have 3 option to operate,
1. Interval Method: Feed your pet in predetermined time intervals.
2. Time Method: Gives you option want to feed your pet at the time you want.
3. Sensor Method: Feed your pet whenever sensor is triggered (need to train your pet).
